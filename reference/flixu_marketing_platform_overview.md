Our mission is simple: **“Born Localized.”** Whether you are a solo developer or an enterprise marketing team, your content should instantly adapt to world markets without breaking your workflow.

## 2. The Core Platform Features (Available Now)

### 🎨 The "Liquid Glass" Workspace
Flixu’s user interface is designed to be stunning, lightning-fast, and completely intuitive. 
* **Zero-Learning Curve:** No cluttered menus or legacy CAT (Computer-Assisted Translation) tool complexity. A premium, seamless workspace that your team will actually love to use.
* **Frictionless Document Parsing (Exact Format Preservation):** Drag and drop `.docx`, `XLIFF`, `.po`, `.yaml`, `.strings`, web-code, or subtitle files. Flixu extracts the text, translates it, and perfectly reconstructs the file. **The exported format is 100% identical to your import.** Your developers don't waste a single second fixing broken tags or layouts.

### 🧠 The Intelligence Layer (Context over Literal Translation)
Generic AI sounds robotic because it lacks context. Flixu’s distinct advantage is its multi-layered Context Engine:
* **The Pre-Translation Analysis Layer:** Before translating a single word, Flixu’s Analysis Engine scans your document to detect the **Domain** (e.g., Legal, SaaS UI, Marketing), **Formality Level** (Formal vs. Casual), and **Target Audience**. This automatically calibrates the LLM's instructions, ensuring the tone is perfect without manual prompting.
* **Advanced Translation Memory (Reference over Replacement):** Traditional CAT tools blindly replace "missing words" in fuzzy matches, creating robotic syntax. Flixu takes a modern approach: We use your TM matches as *dynamic style references*, feeding them to the LLM to guide the translation organically. 
    * *Even if your TM is small*, a Semantic Reranker identifies structurally and conceptually similar past translations (not just literal word matches). This gives the LLM the perfect contextual blueprint to instantly write in your corporate style from day one.
* **Glossary Enforcement:** Say goodbye to wrong terminology. Upload your company’s specific terms (e.g., "Feature", "Dashboard", legal terms), and Flixu strictly enforces their usage across all 22+ supported languages.
* **Brand Voice Manager:** Tell Flixu *how* your brand speaks. Are you playful and casual? Strict and professional? You define the tone once, and the AI natively mimics your exact corporate identity across the globe.

### 🤝 Team Collaboration & Entitlements
* **Enterprise Multi-Tenancy:** Robust workspaces where you can invite your entire team. Assign specific roles like `Project Manager`, `Translator`, or `Admin` to control who can edit glossaries or approve final documents.
* **Smart Metering & Subscriptions:** Transparent credit systems that show exactly what you spend. Effortless scale-up options and an automated Top-Up system ensure your translation pipeline never stalls.

## 3. Advanced Capabilities & "Subtleties" (The Flixu Advantage)

These are the hidden gems that make Flixu feel like magic compared to competitors:
* **Whole-Document Context:** Instead of translating sentence-by-sentence blindly, Flixu reads the *entire* document to understand the overarching narrative before translating a single word.
* **Built-in Quality Estimation (LQA):** Every single translation goes through an automated Linguistic Quality Assurance (LQA) process out-of-the-box. The system scores every segment across multiple dimensions: **Grammar, Accuracy (Meaning Preservation), Terminology Consistency, Formatting (Tags/Placeholders), and Fluency**. Managers know instantly which translations are flawless and which need human attention.
* **Cultural Adaptation Engine (Units & Currencies):** We move beyond word-for-word replacement. Working alongside the translation process, Flixu natively adapts non-linguistic elements like **Currencies, Measurements (metric vs. imperial), Date Formats, and Timezones** to perfectly match the target region's cultural expectations.
* **Image-Aware LLM Context:** Sometimes text alone isn't enough to understand UI buttons or marketing copy. Flixu allows you to pass images—like UI mockups—to the LLM, giving the AI eyes to see exactly where a string lives on the screen.
* **Lightning-Fast Approvals (Slide-Up QA):** Reviewing translations is exceptionally fast. Managers see the source and target side-by-side, can edit seamlessly, and hit "Approve" completely friction-free.
* **Deterministic AI (Zero Hallucinations):** Flixu utilizes advanced backend routing (Qwen & DeepInfra models) structured strictly for translation, preventing the AI from adding commentary or breaking formatting.
* **Scalable Vector Search:** When Flixu searches your past translations for matching phrases, it uses advanced AI vector retrieval (understanding meaning, not just exact keywords) to find the perfect style match in milliseconds.

## 4. Developer Infrastructure & Automation (Zero-Touch Localization)

*Flixu is not just a workspace—it's the ultimate "Zero-Touch" localization infrastructure designed to eliminate manual project management entirely.*

### 🤖 Flixu AI Agents (Vibe Coding Integration)
Flixu breaks out of the dashboard. Using our **Agent Skills**, developers can use Flixu directly inside AI assistants like Claude. Instead of logging into a tool, a user can simply type: *"Translate my new website checkout flow into French using our enterprise brand voice,"* and Flixu handles the rest intelligently in the background.

### 🐙 Headless & Git-Native Localization
Our **GitHub App** eliminates project managers from the loop entirely. 
* A developer writes code in English and pushes it to GitHub. 
* Flixu instantly detects the new text, translates it via your customized Translation Memory and Glossaries, and commits the translated code (Spanish, German, Japanese, etc.) back into the project. 
* **Result:** The software is globally available the second the code is saved.

### ⚡ Zero-Touch Auto-Approval Workflows
To match the speed of modern teams, Flixu utilizes **Rule-Based Automation**. 
* If a translation is a 99% match with your historical memory, or if the internal AI Quality Score (LQA) is over 90/100, Flixu **automatically approves and deploys** the translation. 
* Humans only need to review the small percentage of highly complex, quarantined sentences.

### 🎓 The Post-Edit Learning Loop 
Flixu is a **Self-Learning Engine**. As your human reviewers make small tweaks and corrections to Flixu's translations in the dashboard, the system monitors these edits. It automatically learns your specific stylistic preferences and anti-patterns, constantly updating itself so it never makes the same mistake twice.
