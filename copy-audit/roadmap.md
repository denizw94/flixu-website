### 3. In-Progress
**H2:** What are we building right now?
*Target Frame: Q2 2026*

- **Automated Quality Assurance (LQA) Scoring**
  We are building a secondary evaluation model that automatically reads your translated text and scores it against your [glossary rules](/topics/glossary-terminology-management). It instantly flags any tone deviations or formatting errors, showing managers exactly which sentences need a human review and which are flawless.
- **Dedicated Vector Database Infrastructure**
  As our enterprise Translation Memory scales, we are migrating our semantic search infrastructure to a dedicated vector database. This ensures sub-second retrieval times even when searching through millions of past translation strings.

### 5. Recently Shipped
**H2:** What did we ship recently?
*Deployed: Q1 2026*

- **Multi-format Geometry Parsing:** You can now drag and drop JSON, YAML, or Markdown files. The engine translates the text but mathematically protects the structural code, so your developers never have to fix broken layouts.
- **Vector-based Translation Memory:** The engine now searches your entire translation history for *meaning*, not just exact keyword matches, guaranteeing a consistent brand tone across every document.
- **GitHub App Integration:** A headless CI/CD workflow that automatically translates and commits new strings the second a developer pushes code to the repository. Built for [agile teams](/for/agencies).
- **Brand Voice Configuration Hub:** A centralized dashboard where you can define exactly how your brand speaks (e.g., strictly formal German vs. casual), enforcing these rules globally.
