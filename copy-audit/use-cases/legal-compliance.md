### 4. The Risk of Ambiguity
**H2:** Why is Ambiguity an Operational Risk in Legal Translation?

When translating marketing collateral, creative ambiguity is highly encouraged. When translating an EU Data Processing Agreement (DPA), ambiguity is an operational risk. 

Generic AI translation models are inherently probabilistic; they guess the next most likely word. If a translation conflates "Liability" with "Indemnification" in a German SaaS contract, the legal consequences can be significant.

Historically, the only mitigation for this risk was hiring expensive, specialized legal translation agencies, injecting delays of up to four weeks for critical Terms of Service updates.

### 5. Deterministic Legal Context
**H2:** How Does Flixu Enforce Strict Legal Terminology?

Flixu dramatically reduces the risk of terminology error by binding the AI strictly to your approved legal definitions. Through the [Context Engine](/method), European Data Protection Officers (DPOs) and General Counsel can hardcode exact statutory definitions into the Flixu Terminology Glossary.

If your glossary demands the strictest formal register (e.g., the formal German *Sie*), the AI will output the exact, legally compliant nomenclature every single time.
