# Write or Update Flixu Copy Workflow

Follow these steps exactly when you are asked to write, rewrite, or optimize a page on the Flixu website (Astro, MDX, or Markdown).

1. **Identify the Page Category and Target Audience**
   - Determine if the page is Homepage, Product, Use Case, Topic/Glossary, or Compare.
   - Identify the primary reader: Founder, Product Manager, Localization Lead, Agency, or Freelancer.

2. **Determine the Stage of Awareness**
   - Stage 1-2 (Topics/Glossary): Focus purely on education. Answer the question directly. No hard selling.
   - Stage 2-3 (Homepage, ICP Pages): Start with the PAS Framework. Validate their business problem first, agitate the consequences, then offer Flixu.
   - Stage 3-4 (Product, Method, Compare): Focus on HOW the solution works, differentiation, and benefits over features.

3. **Draft the Content Structure (Answer-First)**
   - Start with a 60–80 word TL;DR or clear definition. No fluff introductions ("In today's fast-paced world...").
   - Break content into H2s/H3s formulated as clear statements or genuine questions.
   - Restrict paragraphs to 60-100 words (one thought per paragraph).

4. **Apply the Brand Voice Check**
   - Ensure the tone is "Calm, Precise, Warm, Human."
   - Remove ALL hype words (revolutionary, game-changing, magic).
   - Verify readability is at a 6th-grade level (Clarity > Cleverness).
   - Ensure the copy is about the *prospect's outcome*, not Flixu's greatness.

5. **Inject SEO & AIO Elements**
   - Insert 3-5 statistics/data points naturally.
   - Add a structured FAQ section if appropriate (5-10 questions, 40-60 word answers).
   - Add the necessary Schema Markup JSON-LD (FAQPage, SoftwareApplication, or Article) if updating the `.astro` code directly.
